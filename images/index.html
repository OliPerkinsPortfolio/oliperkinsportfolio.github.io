Hello! My name is Oli, I am a Games Design Graduate from Bournemouth University in the UK. I am a creative individual with experience in Games Design, 3D Modelling, Texturing and Environmental art. I have been passionate about games and computers for as long as can remember. I have always known that I have a passion for creating games, models and immersive environments for people to play and enjoy.



I love being involved in the games industries communities and learning off other talented individuals. I always like to take extra care in polishing my creations, There is so much to learn in terms of games design and development and it excites me. This is only just the very start of my career in Games and I cannot wait to see where it takes me.

Personal Projects

In my spare time I love to research, New tech, Art styles, Inspiration, Games Development techniques. Other people's creations. Behind the scenes at big AAA companies etc. With this knowledge and inspiration, I create my own projects. These can range in scale; however, I can also use this time to experiment with different industry standard programmes to acquire new skills and techniques.



The goal is that overtime my portfolio is filled with a variety of amazing looking Projects. Recently (which is not shown here) I have been looking into stylized art from low poly games such as World of Warcraft and up to date stylized games such as Valorant. With this information I am trying to learn a variety of techniques on how to model differing art styles, using a wide range of software's such as Photoshop, and Substance Painter to achieve the appropriate look, feel and style required.

University Projects

At University, I have undergone many different units and assignments. Everything from Business Enterprise to Level Design and Modelling.



With the research undertaken from personal projects I pursue in my spare time. I apply this newly found knowledge to my work to enhance and augment my University assignments.



To date, the project I am most proud of is my “Halo ‘Myling” level design. Which can be seen in the image on the right, and more screenshots and a video on my Artstation. This required me to plan out my own multiplayer level. I chose to set my map in the ‘Halo’ universe. It is set in an old abandoned ruin. It took 2 months of planning, grey boxing, modelling in Maya, texturing in Substance Painter, I was able to create my first full map inside a game's engine. I know that I learnt a lot from it all and all future projects I work on will only improve in.

The Future



Although I am still heavily experimenting with games design tools and techniques. In the future I see myself as a 3D Artist/Environmental Artist. This is because I'd love to be responsible for the creation or part creation of the world that surrounds the player. Gameplay wise and graphically. However, I am still very open to new opportunities and challenges in the games industry as I am very passionate about all aspects of the games development and design.
