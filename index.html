3RD Person Prototype (WORK IN PROGRESS / UPDATING)

3RD Person Prototype is a sci-fi stealth shooter set in a galactic war in the year 2323. The player character is a scavenger who is trying to find powerful artifacts in the midst of the galactic conflict.

The gameplay, level design and storytelling is inspired by games like "The Last of Us, Jedi Fallen Order and Uncharted" and features stealth mechanics, sci-fi weapons and a narratively driven story in this broken dystopian setting.

Wizzard VR Speach Recognition Prototype (WORK IN PROGRESS / UPDATING)

This is a VR Sandbox wizard spell casting game This game will allow the player to cast different spells that will interact with the environment all through voice and VR input.

The game will have a handful of spells for the player to experiment with meaning that I will have to create a speech recognition system for the Voice input to understand what words and phrases the player is saying to cast the spell at the tip of the wand the player is holding.

The aim of this experience is to make these control sets work together in new and innovative ways making the player feel like a wizard.
